COVID Daily

COVID Daily is your all know about COVID-19 Patients in India. It's a downloadable project only for Android.

Install Instructions:

  1. Download the "coviddaily.apk" file.
  2. Install the .apk file from your downloads section in your mobile.
  3. If your device blocks the installation, go to settings and enable 'Install from unknown sources.
  4. Use the app and get updated (NOTE: Internet Access required)
  5. Share with others to spread awareness.


Features:

  • Shows data for the Nation along with it's states and union territories. Also includes data in district wise manner.
  • Has a user friendly interface.
  • Follows symptom and precaution details as prescribed by WHO.
  • Pictorial representation of symptoms and precautions.
  • Allows to share data for each state and union territory.
